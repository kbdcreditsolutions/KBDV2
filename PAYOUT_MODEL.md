# KBD Credit Solutions: Connector Payout Model

This model defines how KBD Credit Solutions shares the commissions earned through our primary partnership with **Andromeda** with our network of Connectors/Agents.

## 1. The Payout Flow
1. **Lead Submission:** Connector submits a lead via the KBD Portal.
2. **Bank Login:** KBD passes the lead to Andromeda; Andromeda logs it into the bank.
3. **Disbursal:** Bank disburses the loan.
4. **Andromeda Payout:** Bank pays Andromeda -> Andromeda pays KBD.
5. **Connector Payout:** KBD pays the Connector based on the agreed share.

## 2. Commission Sharing Tiers (Proposed)
We share a percentage of the **gross commission received by KBD from Andromeda**.

| Partner Tier | Monthly Disbursal | Connector Share | KBD Margin |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Bronze** | < ₹50 Lakhs | **60%** | 40% |
| **Silver** | ₹50L - ₹2 Cr | **70%** | 30% |
| **Gold** | ₹2 Cr - ₹5 Cr | **75%** | 25% |
| **Platinum (Elite)**| > ₹5 Cr | **80%** | 20% |

### Example Scenario:
- **Product:** Unsecured Business Loan.
- **Loan Amount:** ₹50,00,000.
- **Andromeda Payout to KBD:** 1.5% = ₹75,000.
- **Partner Tier:** Silver (70% share).
- **Connector Receives:** ₹52,500.
- **KBD Retains:** ₹22,500.

## 3. Payout Cycle & Transparency
- **Standard Cycle:** 15 days after KBD receives the payout from Andromeda.
- **Transparency Tool:** All connectors get a login where they can see:
    - Total Leads Logged.
    - Current Stage (e.g., "Bank Sanction Issued").
    - "Estimated Commission" based on their current tier.

## 4. Why this model scales?
1. **No Fixed Costs:** You only pay when a loan is disbursed.
2. **Incentivized Growth:** Connectors are motivated to hit the next tier to increase their share.
3. **Institutional Support:** Connectors leverage KBD's brand and Andromeda's massive bank network while keeping the majority of the commission.

## 5. CFO Strategy: "The Net Margin"
As a sub-DSA, KBD's goal is to maintain a **Net Margin of 20-30%** across the entire network. This margin covers:
- Private Limited company overheads.
- CRM/Tech subscription costs.
- Sales Manager salaries.
- Marketing for more connectors.
