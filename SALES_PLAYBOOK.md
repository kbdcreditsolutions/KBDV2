# KBD Credit Solutions: Sales Playbook (Stealth Optimization)

Our goal for the next 24 months is to build a massive "Connector-Base" while maintaining operational privacy. This playbook focuses on high-yield partner recruitment.

## 1. The Value Proposition for Connectors
"Access India's largest bank network (Andromeda) via the most transparent platform in the industry (KBD Connect). We provide the tech and the expertise; you provide the leads."

## 2. High-Yield Connector Persona Focus
- **CAs / Tax Consultants:** Focus on "Seamless Unsecured Business Capital for your clients."
- **Real Estate Brokers:** Focus on "Sanctioned Home Loans in < 5 Days."
- **Insurance Agents:** Focus on "Complementary Financial Services to increase your client LTV."

## 3. The "Institutional" Sales Pitch
"KBD Credit Solutions acts as your Master Hub. We bridge the gap between your local leads and 180+ banks through our partnership with Andromeda. Our KBD Connect portal gives you the real-time status visibility that large corporate DSAs often lack. We focus on transparency and reliability."

## 4. Recruitment Funnel (Year 1-2)
### A. The "Vetted" Invitation (Stealth Mode)
Instead of public sign-ups, use a **referral-based sign-up model**.
- Invite 10 "Beta Partners" (CAs/Agents you trust).
- Offer them a "Founder Partner" commission slab (Tier-Up) for the first 6 months.

### B. Scaled Outreach
- LinkedIn outreach to freelance financial consultants using the professional **KBD Connect** landing page.
- "Invite-only" webinars on "Optimizing Loan Disbursals for your clients."

## 5. Retention & Master Partner Velocity
- **Tiered Commissions:** Aggressively move connectors up to the Bronze/Silver/Gold tiers to incentivize volume.
- **Bi-Weekly Updates:** WhatsApp broadcast with interest rate cuts and new bank schemes. (Keep communication professional and platform-branded).

## 6. Closing the Deal (Next Steps)
1. **Invite:** Send a private portal access link.
2. **Onboard:** Instant KYC (Mother's name on the backend logic).
3. **Log Lead:** Aim for the first lead within 48 hours to validate the tech workflow.
