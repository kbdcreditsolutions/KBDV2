# KBD Credit Solutions: Financial Model & Commission Slabs

A successful loan distribution business depends on high-velocity disbursals and fair commission distribution. This model outlines the "CFO Blueprint" for KBD.

## 1. Unit Economics
In the Indian market, banks typically pay a referral fee (payout) ranging from **0.5% to 2.5%** of the loan amount, depending on the product (Home Loan vs. Unsecured Personal Loan).

### Target Average Payouts:
- **Home Loans / LAP:** 0.5% - 0.70%
- **Business Loans (Unsecured):** 1.5% - 2.5%
- **Personal Loans:** 1.0% - 2.0%
- **Credit Cards:** Fixed fee per card (INR 500 - 3000)

## 2. Partner Margin Distribution (Proposed)
To attract connectors, KBD will share a significant portion of the bank's payout.

| Partner Type | Disbursal Range (Monthly) | Payout Share (%) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Connector** | < 1 Cr | 60% of KBD's Payout |
| **Silver Partner** | 1 Cr - 3 Cr | 65% of KBD's Payout |
| **Gold Partner** | 3 Cr - 5 Cr | 70% of KBD's Payout |
| **Diamond Partner** | > 5 Cr | 75% of KBD's Payout |

*Example: If a Connector closes a 1 Cr Business Loan at 2% total payout (2,00,000 INR), the Connector receives 1,20,000 INR and KBD retains 80,000 INR.*

## 4. Operational Burn (OPEX)
- **Tech Maintenance:** Server costs & API integrations.
- **Sales Team:** Salaries for 2-3 Sales Managers to manage partners.
- **Marketing:** Lead generation for the "Connector Program" (Ad spend).
- **Compliance:** Legal fees for DSA tie-ups.

## 5. CFO Recommendations
1. **Payout Cycle:** Standardize on a "Net 15" or "Net 30" cycle relative to when KBD receives the payout from the bank.
2. **Buffer Fund:** Maintain a 10% reserve from profits for "Reversed Commissions" (rare but possible in some bank contracts).
3. **Advanced Payouts:** Once cash flow is stable, offer "Same-day Payout" as a premium feature for Diamond Partners to build extreme loyalty.
