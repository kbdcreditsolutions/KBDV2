The Service Agency Financing Secret: Borrowing Based on Cashflow

April 14, 2026 4 min read KBD Editorial

If you run a digital marketing agency, a consultancy, or a software service firm, you likely don't have massive machinery or real estate to show as collateral. Traditional banks often look away because they don't see physical assets.

The 2026 Reality: Your GST Data is Your Collateral

Lenders are increasingly using Cashflow-Based Underwriting. By analyzing your monthly GST returns and bank statements through our Account Aggregator integration, we can secure unsecured working capital loans based purely on your business's predictable revenue.

Why Cashflow-Based Lending Wins:

  • No Real Estate Needed: Your turnover is the only "security" the bank needs.
  • Dynamic Limits: As your revenue grows, your borrowing limit increases automatically.
  • Speed: Digital verification via GST portals means sanctions in days, not weeks.

Turn your revenue into working capital today. At KBD Credit Solutions, we specialize in cashflow-based lending for the service economy, connecting your verified turnover with lenders who see your growth, not just your assets.
