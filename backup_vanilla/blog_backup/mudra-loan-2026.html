Scaling Your Business with Mudra 2026: Collateral-Free Growth

April 14, 2026 4 min read KBD Editorial

The Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojana (PMMY) remains the backbone of Indian entrepreneurship. In 2026, digital onboarding for Mudra loans has made capital more accessible than ever for small and micro-enterprises.

The Three Tiers of Growth:

  1. Shishu (Loans up to ₹50,000): Ideal for those just starting out—whether it's an e-commerce storefront or a small local service business.
  2. Kishore (Loans up to ₹5 Lakhs): Designed for businesses that have hit their first milestones and need equipment or initial working capital.
  3. Tarun (Loans up to ₹10 Lakhs): For established MSMEs ready to scale operations, hire teams, or expand to new cities.

No Collateral Needed

The true power of Mudra is that it bypasses the need for property-backed security, focusing instead on the potential and viability of your business. Bridge the gap between your vision and your reality with a loan that is as ambitious as you are.
