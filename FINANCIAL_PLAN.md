## 4. CFO Strategic Recommendations
- **Zero-Burn Strategy:** Until the first ₹1 Cr disbursal, keep marketing spend strictly digital and performance-based.
- **Incentive Alignment:** Ensure the Case Manager's bonus is only paid *after* the commission is received from Andromeda.
- **The "2-Year Reservoir":** Reinvest 100% of the first 6 months' profits back into tech development and reserve building for the future Pvt Ltd entity.

## 5. Risk Awareness
- **Andromeda Payout Lag:** Commissions can take 30-60 days. Ensure you have a working capital reserve of ₹1 Lakh to cover Case Manager salaries during the initial payout gap.
