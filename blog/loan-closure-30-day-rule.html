The 30-Day Rule: Your Rights After Loan Closure

April 14, 2026 3 min read KBD Editorial

Closing a home loan should be a celebration, not a bureaucratic nightmare. Many borrowers are unaware that under RBI guidelines, banks must return all original property documents within 30 days of loan closure.

If they fail? They owe you.

The RBI has set strict penalties for banks that delay the return of your property papers:

  • The Penalty: ₹5,000 for every day of delay beyond the 30-day window.
  • Document Safety: Banks must also compensate you for any lost or damaged original documents.
  • Why it matters: Your property papers are your most valuable asset. Banks holding them "hostage" after you've paid in full is not just frustrating—it's illegal.
