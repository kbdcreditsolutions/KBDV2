# KBD Credit Solutions: Partner/Connector Agreement (Template)

**DATE:** [Insert Date]
**BETWEEN:** KBD Credit Solutions (Represented by [Nominated Owner Name])

---

### 1. Scope of Services
The Connector agrees to refer potential loan customers (Personal, Home, Business, Professional Loans) to KBD Credit Solutions. KBD Credit Solutions will facilitate the processing of these leads through its institutional banking network (including our partnership with Andromeda Sales & Distribution Pvt Ltd).

### 2. Payout Structure (Commission Sharing)
KBD Credit Solutions agrees to share a percentage of the net commission received from the bank/distributor with the Connector as per the following slabs:

| Disbursal Volume (per Month) | Connector Share | KBD Share |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Up to ₹50 Lakhs | 60% of Net Payout | 40% |
| ₹50 Lakhs - ₹1.5 Crores | 70% of Net Payout | 30% |
| Above ₹1.5 Crores | 80% of Net Payout | 20% |

*Note: "Net Payout" refers to the commission received by KBD Credit Solutions after any statutory deductions (TDS, etc.) by the bank or master distributor.*

### 3. Roles & Responsibilities
- **Connector:** Responsible for sourcing leads and providing accurate primary documentation (KYC, Income proof).
- **KBD Credit Solutions:** Responsible for file logging, bank coordination, status tracking, and ensuring timely commission payouts.

### 4. Payout Timeline
- Commissions reached by KBD Credit Solutions between the 1st and 30th of a month will be disbursed to the Connector by the **15th of the following month**.
- Payments will be made via NEFT/IMPS/UPI to the Connector’s registered bank account.

### 5. Transparency & Technology
The Connector will be provided with access to the **KBD Connect Portal** for real-time tracking of lead statuses. Any discrepancies must be reported within 48 hours of status update.

### 6. Confidentiality & Data Privacy
Both parties agree to protect the privacy of customer data. The Connector shall not utilize lead data for any purpose other than referring it to KBD Credit Solutions.

### 7. Termination
Either party may terminate this agreement with a 15-day written notice. Any pending payouts for disbursed files will be honored post-termination.
