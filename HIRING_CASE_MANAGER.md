# KBD Credit Solutions: Case Manager Hiring Guide

To scale KBD Credit Solutions V2, you need an operational expert who handles the "Logistics" of loan processing while you focus on "Strategy and Growth." This role is the backbone of your partnership with Andromeda.

## 1. Job Description: Loan Case Manager
**Objective:** Manage the end-to-end lifecycle of loan applications from connector submission to final disbursal.

### Key Responsibilities:
- **Lead Verification:** Review documents submitted by connectors for completeness and accuracy (Aadhar, PAN, IT Returns, Bank Statements).
- **Andromeda Coordination:** Manually log verified leads into the Andromeda Portal or hand them over to the assigned Andromeda Relationship Manager.
- **Status Management:** Track every lead's status daily and update the **KBD Connect Portal**.
- **Connector Support:** Act as the first point of contact for agents when they have questions about their leads.
- **Document Collection:** Follow up with end-customers or connectors if banks require additional documentation.

## 2. Ideal Candidate Profile
- **Experience:** 1-3 years in the loan/banking department of a DSA or bank.
- **Knowledge:** Familiarity with CIBIL reports, basic income-to-loan ratios, and lead status terminologies (Login, Sanction, Disbursement).
- **Software Skills:** Proficient in Excel, WhatsApp Web, and comfortable learning the custom KBD Portal.
- **Soft Skills:** High attention to detail (missing documents lead to rejections).

## 3. Hiring Strategy (Immediate)
- **Sourcing:** Use portals like Portals like Naukri, Indeed, or local FB groups for "Loan Agents/DSA Staff."
- **Interview Questions:**
    - "Can you name 5 common documents required for an Unsecured Business Loan?"
    - "How do you handle a situation where a bank rejects a lead due to a low CIBIL score?"
    - "Explain the difference between a 'Sanction Letter' and a 'Disbursement'."

## 4. Compensation & Budget (Estimates)
- **Base Salary:** ₹15,000 - ₹25,000 per month (depending on experience).
- **Incentive:** ₹500 - ₹1,000 per successful disbursement as a "Success Bonus." 
    - *Why:* This aligns their speed with your revenue.

## 5. Training Plan (Week 1)
- **Day 1:** Overview of KBD Credit Solutions V2 and the Andromeda relationship.
- **Day 2:** Training on the **KBD Connector Portal** and the Lead Submission flow.
- **Day 3-5:** Shadowing your current manual process of handing leads to Andromeda.
