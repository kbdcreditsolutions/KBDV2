# KBD Credit Solutions: Monthly Financial & Ops Routine

To build a professional Private Limited company in 2 years, you must start acting like one today. This monthly routine ensures clarity, tax compliance, and financial health.

## 1. Financial Closing (Every 1st - 5th)
- [ ] **Andromeda Reconciliation:** Match the "Disbursal Report" from Andromeda against your internal Lead List.
- [ ] **Invoicing:** Ensure all invoices for the previous month's commissions have been submitted by your CA.
- [ ] **Profit & Loss (P&L):** Use Zoho Books or Excel to track:
    - *Revenue:* Commissions received from Andromeda.
    - *Expenses:* Case Manager salary + incentives, Tech (Vercel/Supabase), Marketing (Ads).
- [ ] **Tax Reserve:** Set aside 20-30% of your net profit into a separate "Tax Savings" bucket for future income tax payments.

## 2. Partner Payouts (Every 10th - 15th)
- [ ] **Connector Audit:** Calculate the total payout due to each connector based on their [Payout Slab](file:///Users/manojaaa/Desktop/KBD%20Credit%20Solutions/PAYOUT_MODEL.md).
- [ ] **Transfer:** Execute payments from the business current account.
- [ ] **Communication:** Send professional "Commission Statements" (PDFs) to each connector via WhatsApp/Email.

## 3. Operations & Strategy (Every 25th)
- [ ] **Conversion Audit:** Review "Lead to Login" and "Login to Disbursal" ratios. If "Lead to Login" is low, your connectors need better training.
- [ ] **Case Manager Review:** Review the TAT (Turnaround Time) for your Case Manager’s file processing.
- [ ] **Goal Setting:** Define the disbursal target for the next month (e.g., Target: ₹1 Cr).

## 4. CA Coordination Checklist
- [ ] Share all bank statements (PDF/Excel) with your CA.
- [ ] Provide details of all TDS deducted by banks/Andromeda.
- [ ] Ensure GST returns (GSTR-1 & GSTR-3B) are filed on time to maintain a clean track record for future loans.
