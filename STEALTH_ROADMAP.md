# KBD Credit Solutions: V2 Fresh Start Roadmap (24 Months)

This roadmap focuses on restarting KBD Credit Solutions as a high-authority hub, scaling from the current ₹10-20 Lakhs to ₹10 Crore+ monthly disbursal volume.

## Phase 1: V2 Foundation & Ops (Months 1-4)
**Goal:** Professionalize the identity and hire the first operator.
- [ ] **Identity Revamp:** Keep "KBD Credit Solutions." Update logo and communication style to "Institutional Navy/Gold."
- [ ] **Case Manager Hiring:** Onboard a dedicated operator to handle Andromeda coordination and portal status updates.
- [ ] **Bookkeeping Boot:** Implement the [Monthly Routine](file:///Users/manojaaa/Desktop/KBD%20Credit%20Solutions/MONTHLY_ROUTINE.md) using Zoho Books/Tally.
- [ ] **Tech Initialization:** Start development of the custom **KBD Connect** platform (Next.js).

## Phase 2: Tech Deployment & First Connectors (Months 5-10)
**Goal:** Launch the platform and onboard the first 10 core partners.
- [ ] **Portal Launch:** Members-only hub for connectors to log leads and track status.
- [ ] **WhatsApp Sync:** Automated status pings from KBD to connectors.
- [ ] **Target:** Achieve ₹1 Cr monthly disbursal volume.
- [ ] **Connector Onboarding:** Targeted outreach to CAs and local property agents.

## Phase 3: Scaling the Network (Months 11-18)
**Goal:** Aggressive growth and Tier-Up with Andromeda.
- [ ] **Ad spend:** Dedicated LinkedIn/Meta budget for "Connector Recruitment."
- [ ] **Andromeda Tier-Up:** Negotiate higher commission slabs by hitting ₹5 Cr+ monthly volume.
- [ ] **Operational Scale:** Hire a second Case Manager if TAT (Turnaround Time) exceeds 24 hours.

## Phase 4: Corporate Pivot (Months 19-24)
**Goal:** Transition to Private Limited.
- [ ] **Structure:** Partners (You and your partner) become the face/directors.
- [ ] **Migration:** Transfer tech, data, and partner agreements to the new entity.
- [ ] **Target:** ₹10 Cr - ₹15 Cr monthly disbursal with a 2-year audited track record.
