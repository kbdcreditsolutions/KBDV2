# KBD Credit Solutions: Case Manager Standard Operating Procedure (SOP)

This manual outlines the daily responsibilities and workflows for the KBD Case Manager to ensure high conversion rates and partner satisfaction.

## 1. Daily Morning Routine (9:30 AM - 10:30 AM)
- [ ] **Andromeda Sync:** Log into the Andromeda Portal and check for status updates on all active files.
- [ ] **KBD Dashboard Refresh:** Update the status of leads in the **KBD Connect Portal** based on the Andromeda data.
- [ ] **WhatsApp Catch-up:** Respond to connector queries and customer follow-ups from the previous evening.

## 2. Lead Intake & Verification
When a new lead is submitted via the KBD Portal:
1. **Primary Check:** Call the customer within 30 minutes to verify details.
2. **Document Checklist:** Ensure the following are collected (digitally):
    - **Salaried:** 3 mo Salary slips, 6 mo Bank stmt, PAN, Aadhar.
    - **Self-Employed:** 2 yrs ITR, 12 mo Bank stmt, Business Proof, PAN, Aadhar.
3. **Credit Filter:** Run a soft credit check (if enabled) or check for bounce history in the bank statement.

## 3. The "Andromeda Handover"
Once a lead is verified by KBD:
- **Logging:** Enter the details into the Andromeda Partner Portal under the KBD code.
- **Assignment:** Assign the file to the relevant Andromeda RM (Relationship Manager).
- **Tracking:** Note down the "Andromeda File ID" and link it to the KBD Lead ID.

## 4. Status Terminology Mapping
To ensure transparency for our connectors, use the following mapping:

| Bank/Andromeda Status | KBD Portal Status | Action Required |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Login / Applied | **File Logged** | Inform Connector. |
| Verification / PD | **In Progress** | Customer to expect a verification call/visit. |
| Sanctioned | **Approved** | Send Sanction Letter to Connector. |
| Disbursed | **Disbursed** | Trigger Commission Accrual. |
| Technical / Legal Reject | **On Hold** | Request additional property docs. |

## 5. Payout Reconciliation (Monthly)
On the 1st of every month:
- **Download:** Andromeda's Disbursement Report.
- **Verify:** Match with KBD's internal Disbursal list.
- **Escalate:** Any missing disbursals to the Andromeda BH (Branch Head) immediately.

## 6. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- **TAT (Turnaround Time):** Lead logged in Andromeda within 4 hours of submission to KBD.
- **Accuracy:** Zero rejections due to "missing primary documentation."
- **Communication:** Every "Sanction" notification sent to the Connector via WhatsApp within 15 minutes of update.
